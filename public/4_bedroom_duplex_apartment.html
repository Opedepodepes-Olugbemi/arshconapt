2 units of 4 bedroom semi detached duplex at barrack busstop, Soluyi, Gbagada, Lagos state.

We're proud to be a part of creating a spectacular structure and to continue in developing building, that supports this vision.

Nestled within the prestigious Bashorun neighborhood in Ibadan, the architectural marvel in the Engr. Lere Adigun GRA Estate stands as a testament to timeless design and meticulous craftsmanship. Here, eight semi-detached duplexes, each boasting four bedrooms, weave together luxury, functionality, and a touch of enchantment.

    These duplexes are more than mere structures; they are lyrical compositions. The interplay of light and shadow dances across their facades, revealing intricate details—a wrought-iron balcony here, a bay window there. - Engr. Lere Adigun's vision harmonizes contemporary aesthetics with nods to classical elegance. The result? A symphony of lines, curves, and angles that captivates the eye.

    How Can I contact Arshcon?

    You can visit our website and use the contact form to send us a message. Simply provide your first name, last name, email address, and the subject of your inquiry.

    What projects are you interested in?

    At Arshcon, we are passionate about a diverse range of projects. Our interests span across innovative architectural designs, sustainable building solutions, and cutting-edge technology integrations. We thrive on challenges that push the boundaries of creativity and functionality, ensuring each project is unique and impactful. Whether it’s residential, commercial, or public infrastructure, we are committed to delivering excellence and innovation in every endeavor.
